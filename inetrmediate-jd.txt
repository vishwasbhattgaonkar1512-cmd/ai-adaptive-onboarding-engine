Job Title: Associate Data & Reporting Analyst

Location: Pune, Maharashtra, India
Employment Type: Full-Time

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for an Associate Data & Reporting Analyst who can support internal analytics, dashboarding, and reporting workflows. The ideal candidate should have a solid foundation in SQL, Python, Excel, and visualization tools, along with the ability to work with APIs, structured datasets, and cross-functional teams. This role is ideal for a candidate with internship or project experience who wants to grow into a product and analytics-focused position.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Build and maintain dashboards for internal reporting and performance tracking.
- Write SQL queries to extract, clean, and transform data from operational databases.
- Use Python for data preparation, reporting support, and lightweight automation tasks.
- Work with Excel and BI tools to create clear business reports and visual summaries.
- Support product, operations, and business teams with data-backed insights.
- Assist in integrating API-based data sources into reporting workflows.
- Participate in internal tooling and onboarding optimization projects.
- Communicate analytical findings clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
